<p><p>The Anti-sniper Detection System market comprises three primary technologies: infrared, laser, and acoustic. Infrared systems detect heat signatures from human bodies and weapon discharge, effectively spotting snipers at range. Laser detection systems use beams to identify the precise origin of gunfire, often triangulating positions quickly. Acoustic systems analyze sound waves generated by gunfire to pinpoint location, relying on advanced algorithms to differentiate between various noises. Together, these types enhance situational awareness and improve counter-sniper measures in defense operations.</p></p>

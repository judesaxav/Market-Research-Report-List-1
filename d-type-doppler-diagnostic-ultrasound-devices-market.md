<p><p>D type Doppler Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices include Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed Wave (PW) Doppler ultrasound technologies. CW Doppler uses a continuous sound wave to measure high-velocity blood flow, making it ideal for assessing cardiac conditions, while PW Doppler emits short bursts of sound to provide depth-specific flow measurements, enabling detailed analysis of lower-velocity blood flow in various tissues. Each type serves distinct clinical applications, contributing to a comprehensive understanding of cardiovascular health and overall diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>

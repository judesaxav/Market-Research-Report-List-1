<p><p>Student Information Systems (SIS) software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and web-based. Cloud-based SIS operates on remote servers, allowing users to access data and features from any device with an internet connection. This often leads to reduced IT costs and automatic updates. In contrast, web-based SIS is accessible through internet browsers but may require installation on local servers. Both types facilitate efficient management of student data, but cloud-based solutions typically offer greater scalability and flexibility.</p></p>

<p><p>The mechanical keyboard shaft market consists mainly of two types: linear shaft type and tactile shaft type. Linear shafts provide a smooth keystroke without any tactile feedback, ideal for gamers who prefer swift key presses. Tactile shafts, on the other hand, offer a noticeable bump at the actuation point, enhancing typing accuracy and providing feedback to the user, making them popular among typists. Both types cater to distinct user preferences, influencing their respective demand in the market.</p></p>

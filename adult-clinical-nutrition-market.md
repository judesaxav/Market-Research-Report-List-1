<p><ul><li>Oral Nutrition</li><li>Enteral Nutrition</li><li>Parenteral Nutrition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Adult Clinical Nutrition market is categorized into three main types: Oral Nutrition, Enteral Nutrition, and Parenteral Nutrition. Oral Nutrition encompasses dietary supplements designed for individuals who can consume food but need additional nutrients. Enteral Nutrition involves delivering nutrients directly to the gastrointestinal tract, typically through tubes for those who cannot eat normally. Parenteral Nutrition provides essential nutrients intravenously, bypassing the digestive system entirely, suitable for patients with severe malnutrition or gastrointestinal disorders. Each type addresses distinct patient needs for optimal health.</p></p>
